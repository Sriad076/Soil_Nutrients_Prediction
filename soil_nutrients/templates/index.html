Soil Nutrients Prediction

A machine learning based regression model to predict basic soil nutrient.

Data Collection

We collectes images from the Landsat-8 satellite. It contains two sensors, first sensor collects 6 bands multi-spectral imagery at 30-meter resolution, as well as panchromatic imagery at 15-meter. And second sensor collects thermal imagery at 100 meters resolution. Images were obtained from 2020 to 2021.

Pre-Processing

Collect the relevant data from `DIVA GIS’ website before using the ArcMap software on your PC. Example: The `BangladeshMap.shp’ data can be downloaded by selecting `Bangladesh’ and `Administrative areas’ as the subject. From the ArcToolbox shelf, choose and click’
Analyst Tools → Extraction → Extractbymask.

What is our goal?

Our objective is to produce a good online application that will allow farmers to correctly use their property and improve their soil.
